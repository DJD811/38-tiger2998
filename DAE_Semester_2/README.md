# Semester 2: Workforce Readiness

This folder contains materials and projects related to my Semester 2 focus on workforce readiness. This phase of the tech cohort is dedicated to developing professional skills and building an online presence essential for a successful career in tech.

## Contents

-   **GitHub Pages**: Resources and files related to setting up and maintaining my personal GitHub Pages for portfolio showcasing.
-   **LinkedIn Profile Development**: Strategies, drafts, and assets used to optimize my LinkedIn profile.
-   **Resumes**: Various versions of my resume, tailored for different job applications, highlighting skills and experiences.
-   **Elevator Pitch**: Scripts and practice materials for my elevator pitch, designed to effectively communicate my skills and aspirations.
-   **Other Professional Development**: Any additional documents or projects contributing to my workforce readiness.

## Purpose

The primary purpose of this folder is to house all artifacts that demonstrate my preparedness for the professional tech landscape. It's a key component of my overall portfolio, showcasing not just technical skills but also professional presentation and communication abilities.
