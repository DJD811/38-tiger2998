# My Journey into Cybersecurity

Hello! I'm Denis, a driven professional with a background in Computer Science and a passion for reading in between the lines wherever they may be either in a book or on a computer screen. My journey has led me from the class rooms of Eletrical Engineering into the dynamic field of Cybersecurity, where I am now training to become an Ethical Hacker.

## From Data to Defense

My experience working in python provided for me at the University of Conneticut has provided me with a strong foundation in coding. While reading horror thrillers and fantasy novels has helped me in learning to pay attention to details and critical thinking. My interests in forensics shows and psychology has drawn me to idea of solving problems through the science process, and I am now applying that same passion to identifying and mitigating security threats.

This career pivot is being guided by a rigorous six-month tech cohort, where I am gaining hands-on experience with the tools and methodologies used by today's top security professionals. My goal is to leverage my analytical background and these new, specialized skills to help organizations protect their most critical assets from cyber threats.

## My Mission

I am committed to becoming a valuable asset in a Security Operations Center (SOC) environment. I thrive under pressure and am eager to contribute to a team that is on the front lines of cyber defense. My objective is to help organizations minimize the impact of security breaches and ensure a swift return to normal operations.

*   **Analytical Problem-Solving**: Ability to dissect complex problems and identify root causes.
*   **High-Pressure Performance**: Adept at working in fast-paced environments to meet critical deadlines (SLAs).
*   **Strong Communication**: Skilled in documenting technical findings and explaining risks to diverse stakeholders.
