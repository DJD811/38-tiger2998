# Prompt Engineering Studies

This `prompt_engineering_1` folder is dedicated to exploring and documenting various aspects of prompt engineering. It serves as a sandbox for experimenting with different prompting techniques, strategies, and their outcomes, especially in the context of interacting with large language models or AI systems.

## Contents

-   **Prompt Examples**: A collection of prompts designed for various tasks and models.
-   **Experiment Logs**: Documentation of prompt variations, model responses, and analysis of effectiveness.
-   **Research & Notes**: Articles, summaries, and personal notes on prompt engineering best practices and theory.
-   **Tools/Scripts**: Any scripts or tools developed to assist in prompt generation, evaluation, or interaction.

## Purpose

The purpose of this `prompt_engineering_1` folder is to deepen my understanding and practical skills in prompt engineering. It acts as a learning hub to refine the art and science of communicating effectively with AI, a crucial skill in modern AI-driven development.
